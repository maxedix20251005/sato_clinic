診察内容Services

一般内科診療General Internal Medicine

風邪や発熱、喉の痛み、頭痛、腹痛などの一般的な体調不良に幅広く対応します。軽い症状でも早期診断に努め、安心して受診いただける体制を整えています。We treat common conditions such as colds, fever, sore throat, headaches, and stomach pain. Even mild symptoms are assessed promptly to provide reassurance and early diagnosis.

生活習慣病の診断・治療Lifestyle‑related Chronic Care

高血圧、糖尿病、脂質異常症、メタボリック症候群などを継続管理し、生活指導と薬物療法で進行予防を図ります。We manage hypertension, diabetes, dyslipidemia, and metabolic syndrome with continuous follow-up, lifestyle guidance, and medications to prevent progression.

消化器系の診察Gastroenterology

胃痛や胸やけ、便秘・下痢などの消化器症状に対応し、必要に応じて内視鏡や超音波検査を行います。We address digestive issues such as stomach pain, heartburn, constipation, and diarrhea, and perform endoscopy or ultrasound when indicated.

呼吸器系の診察Respiratory Care

咳・息切れ・胸痛などに対応し、喘息やCOPD、アレルギー性鼻炎などの管理と予防をサポートします。We evaluate cough, shortness of breath, and chest discomfort, and support management and prevention of asthma, COPD, and allergic rhinitis.
